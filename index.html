Welcome to CYG

Where unforgettable memories are made every summer. Our day camp combines Torah values, exciting activities, and lifelong friendships in a safe, nurturing environment.

Our Mission

Providing boys with an enriching summer experience that combines fun, learning, and personal growth in a Torah-centered environment.

Location

Based in Lakewood, NJ with amazing facilities including sports courts, activity rooms, and outdoor spaces perfect for all our programs.

Schedule

Full day program running throughout the summer with flexible scheduling options to fit your family's needs.

Activities

Sports, arts & crafts, swimming, trips, special events, and so much more. Every day brings new excitement and adventure!

Expert Staff

Our dedicated counselors and specialists ensure every camper receives personal attention and has an incredible summer.

Safe Environment

Safety is our top priority. All staff are carefully screened, trained, and committed to creating a secure, positive atmosphere.

Our Divisions

We organize campers by age and grade to ensure age-appropriate activities and optimal social experiences for everyone.

Tzeirei Govoha

Grades 2-3

Our youngest division focuses on building confidence, making friends, and discovering new interests. Activities are specially designed for our younger campers with extra supervision and age-appropriate programming that keeps them engaged and excited all day long.

Bnei Govoha

Grades 4-6

The heart of our camp! This division offers the perfect balance of structured activities and free choice time. Campers develop independence, leadership skills, and lasting friendships through sports tournaments, creative projects, and exciting trips.

Bachurei Govoha

Grades 7-8

Our oldest division emphasizes leadership, responsibility, and meaningful experiences. Bachurei Govoha campers enjoy more challenging activities, special privileges, and opportunities to be role models while still having an amazing, fun-filled summer.
